# Commercial Frameworks

This section contains practical commercial frameworks, methodologies and decision models developed through executive leadership roles across international organizations.

The frameworks are designed to help business leaders evaluate complex commercial situations, structure transformation initiatives and make strategic decisions.

Unlike academic models, these frameworks are based on real-world commercial experience across multinational companies, international expansion projects and commercial transformation programs.
