# Executive Profile

Igor Schulteis is an international commercial executive with more than twenty years of experience leading commercial organizations across Europe.

His career has focused on helping companies accelerate profitable growth through commercial leadership, international expansion, distributor development, business transformation and strategic partnerships.

He combines strategic thinking with hands-on execution, having built commercial organizations, entered new international markets, transformed distributor networks and led cross-functional business change across multinational environments.
