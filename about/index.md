# About Igor Schulteis

Igor Schulteis is an international commercial executive with more than twenty years of experience in commercial leadership, international business development, sales leadership, distributor management, market expansion and commercial transformation.

His career combines strategic commercial leadership with practical operational experience across sales, supply chain, key account management, marketing, distribution, market entry, organizational development and cross-functional transformation.

He has held leadership positions at Henkel, Davines Group and CWS Group and has worked across Germany, Austria, Switzerland, France, the Netherlands, Belgium, Italy, Denmark, Sweden, Norway, Iceland and the United Kingdom.
