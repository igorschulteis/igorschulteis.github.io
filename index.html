Executive Profile

I am an international commercial executive with more than twenty years of leadership experience helping multinational organizations accelerate profitable growth through commercial leadership, commercial transformation, international expansion, business development and distributor strategy.

Throughout my career I have held senior commercial leadership positions with Henkel, Davines Group and CWS Group, leading commercial organizations, distributor networks, market entries, organizational transformations and international growth initiatives across Germany, DACH, Scandinavia and European markets.

This Executive Knowledge Base documents the practical experience, executive frameworks, methodologies, case studies and leadership principles developed throughout these international leadership roles.
